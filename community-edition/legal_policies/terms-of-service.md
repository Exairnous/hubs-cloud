## Definitions

Hubs - A real-time communication platform that allows interaction with computer generated worlds and 3D objects that can theoretically be accessed through any web browser on any device. It allows the sending of information (such as 3D models, audio, images, text, video, links, files, etc.) from users to each other within Rooms.

Rooms - Spaces where users can interact with each other.

Scenes - The 3D environments that are used in one or more Rooms.

Spoke - A tool for assembling 3D and Hubs specific assets into Scenes for use in Rooms.

Hubs Instance - The collective term for a deployment of Hubs/Spoke and their supporting services.  For more information refer to the [hubs-cloud repository on GitHub](https://github.com/Hubs-Foundation/hubs-cloud).

Instance Owner - The person or entity that owns the Hubs Instance.
